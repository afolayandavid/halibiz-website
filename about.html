About Us



Halibiz Industries was founded in the year 2020 and is located in Nigeria at Plot No. 1361 CAD Zone C16 Industrial Area 1 and Extension in the Federal Capital Territory (FCT) Abuja. Halibiz Industries has five (5) major complexes comprising of the Administrative Block and four (4) factory buildings (Factory A, B, C, & D). Each of these factories carry the approved NAFDAC requirements to meet up with the ever challenging need of food production standard, and we are operating at international standards for industrial food production.





The administrative block is made up of a two storey building which comprises of several offices with an inclusion of a large conference room, a highly visible and illuminating show room and many other compartments. The factories are home to the latest machines and equipment required for the production of healthy, high quality products to Nigerians and the rest of the world. The Industry is geared towards automation with its improved and modernized production equipment which enhances food processing and ensures that the products meet the international best practices and also the desirability of its esteem customers.

The Industry houses a growing number of top-notch professionals which comprises of individuals with proven set of skills and experience in their various professions relevant to each department of the Industry. This team of professionals is led by The Vice President, Mr. Adeniyi Joel Ojo.



Mr Ojo's profile includes a high level of professionalism in Factory Management, Quality Assurance and a certified Food Safety Auditor. He possess a Bachelor of Technology in Food Science and Nutrition at the Federal University of Technology, Minna. He also has a Masters Degree in Human Nutrition from the College of Medicine, Faculty of Public Health, University of Ibadan. He has an unmatched working experience with other industries such as Yale foods, Boulos Foods, Oriental foods and many more. His career pride is on strategic teamwork and effective collaboration, which is the hallmark of his career and leadership style.

It is not an exaggerating to say that with the Industry's modern and up-to-date technology and equipment, alongside a well-balanced team of professionals headed by a qualified and able Vice President, the Industry projects a high inflow which will recoup the Industry’s capital investments in no distant time. The company's future aspiration is to see that Nigeria is healthier with the consumption of one or more product from its factories per day by every family in Nigeria before 2022 and in the process impacting on the community by doing it our own way, through the creation of opportunity to Nigerians and thereby improving the Nigerian economy.
