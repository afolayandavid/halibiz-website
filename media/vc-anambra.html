Visit of the Vice Chancellor of Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University, Uli.

Visit of the Vice Chancellor of Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University, Uli.
04th February, 2021

04th February, 2021





We were pleased to recieve The Vice Chancellor of Chukwu Odumegu University, Prof. Greg Chukwudi Nwakoby and his entourage in Halibiz Industries on the 4th of February, 2021. The Vice Chancellor has put in a lot of years in public service, with acumulated experience both in the educational and business sectors. In that regard, he was thrilled on the progress of the industry to the great heights it is today. He also expressed how excited he was to be in Halibiz Industries and anticipated the launch of Halibiz Products to the Nigerian market.





He further went on a tour around the facilities and had a taste of some of the products we produce. In his final remarks of the day, the Vice Chancellor enunciated his interest in doing business with Halibiz Industries in the nearest future. We thanked and appreciated him for his visit while he left with his entourage.
